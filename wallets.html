Wallets

Official

Kalium - Send BANANO to all your friends on the go with Kalium. Available on iOS & Android.

The Banano Stand - Browser-based lightwallet that also supports Ledger Nano S. Ideal for desktop.

  • Kalium
  • The Banano Stand

Paper Wallet Generator - Gift BANANO to friends, family or strangers. Choose a design, click "Generate", print the page and load the account with funds.

Camo-Banano Light Wallet - A light wallet for Banano that supports camo (privacy-focused) transactions.

  • Paper Wallet Generator
  • Camo-Banano

Tipping Services

Banano Discord Tipbot - Ideal for small amounts, to tip and get tipped on Discord. It is fully managed.

Banano Reddit Tipbot - Ideal for small amounts, to tip and get tipped on Reddit. It is fully managed.

  • Discord Tipbot
  • Reddit Tipbot

Tip.cc - A 3rd party multi-chain Discord tipping service that supports BANANO. Send and receive BAN on your favorite Discord servers.

Wrkzcoin TipBot - A 3rd party multi-chain Discord tipping service that supports BANANO.

cctip_bot - A 3rd party multi-chain Telegram tipping service that supports BANANO. Provided by CWallet
